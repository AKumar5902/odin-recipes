Fries!

Your weird if you don't like frigit add reces

Ingredients:

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt or any seasoning of your choice

Steps:

  1. Slice the potatoes
  2. Soak in cold water for at least an hour or more
  3. Rinse them twice and pat them dry
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees for about 5-6 min
  5. Increase oil to 400 and fry until golden brown
  6. Place on paper towel and sprinkle salt or whatever seasoning you want
